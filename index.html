About Us

The success history of Sumudran over 100 years

Fulfilling Unique Printing and Publishing Needs with Precision

We take immense pride in introducing ourselves as the pioneers of printing in Jharkhand, a legacy that began in 1912, followed by the establishment of our offset unit in 1981. Over the years, our commitment has been to deliver specialized printing and publishing services that consistently meet the unique needs of various organizations, leaving them entirely satisfied. In order to effectively address our client's requirements and timelines, we ensure our arsenal is stocked with the latest design, textsetting, processing, printing, and finishing machinery, operated by skilled specialists and a dedicated workforce. Our comprehensive single-point facility empowers us to provide prompt, top-notch, and precise services.
